Lead Flow: 4 Funnels

21 students/month needs roughly 100+ completed Typeform applications per month. These four funnels feed that pipeline. Funnels 1-3 are ready now or during Cohort 1. Funnel 4 is built from Cohort 1 data.

Funnel 1 - Existing Buyers

Store Buyers > Welcome Sequence > Mentorship

Every sample pack, preset pack, or Ableton rack buyer gets put onto a Klaviyo welcome sequence that nurtures them toward the mentorship. They've already paid Rob - now deepen the relationship.

Signal-to-noise: Lower. Many buyers are hobbyists or younger producers. But volume is high and the sequence filters automatically - serious producers self-select in, brokies self-select out at $3k.

Signal-to-noise: Highest. YouTube viewers invest serious time watching Rob. They understand his approach and self-qualify by watching full videos. Also evergreen - videos keep generating applications months after upload.

Paid Ads > $97-197 Product > Nurture Sequence > Mentorship

Key Decisions After Cohort 1
  • Did the mix surgery work? If students loved it, make it weekly. If not, try a different format (you hosting, or a different specialist).
  • What were the top 10 questions in WhatsApp? These become either FAQ recordings, Blueprint module updates, or the low-ticket product (Funnel 4).
  • Is $3,000 the right price? If Cohort 1 fills easily and testimonials are strong, $3k is conservative. If it's a harder sell, test $2,497 with a higher cohort size.
  • What's the middle tier? Cohort 1 data tells you if there's a $697-997 "Blueprint + community access" product between the self-serve Blueprint and the full mentorship.
  • Should cohorts overlap? If demand exceeds supply by Cohort 3, stagger start dates so a new cohort begins every 3-4 weeks instead of waiting 6.
